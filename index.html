About M-Core

M-CORE Electrical Services Inc. is a London based company servicing Southwestern Ontario. We have built our reputation by providing excellent workmanship and prompt, reliable customer service to the residential, commercial, industrial and medical sectors. All of our journeyman electricians are fully licensed and insured, ensuring our clients receive the best service possible.

Our philosophy is to implement effective solutions in the most professional manner to meet our customer’s needs.

M-Core Residential Services

M-CORE is able to manage all aspects of your residential electrical system.

Our installation and maintenance services include, but are not limited to:

• Service upgrades

• Fuse panel replacement

• Aluminum, knob & tube wiring replacement

• Hot tub connections

• Code corrections

• Toubleshooting

• Additions & renovations

• Pot light installation

• Whole home sure protection

• Decorative lighting



M-Core Commercial Services

M-CORE is able to manage all aspects of workplace electrical systems.

Our installation and maintenance services include, but are not limited to:

• Provision of cost savings analysis and access to rebate programs for interior & exterior lighting upgrades

• Emergency & exit lighting installation & testing

• Fire alarm installation

• Additions & renovations

• Electrical systems & equipment troubleshooting

• Sure protection for sensitive electronic equipment troubleshooting

• On call service availability


M-Core Medical Services

M-CORE has the experience and knowledge necessary to manage the special electrical requirements of patient care areas.

We are capable of installing and servicing the electrical systems of medical, dental and optometry clinics. Our installation and maintenance services include, but are not limited to:

• Supply and installation of UPS and emergency power back-up systems


• Reception, office and treatment area lighting

• After hours service to accomodate clinic scheduling

• On call service availability
